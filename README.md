### About

We are **Leandro** and **Damian** – two 18-year-old apprentices from Switzerland. For the past two years, we have been coding with deep passion. We joined forces as **dev-aid** to build tools that give back to the developer community.

Our mission is to create public tools that simplify the lives of fellow programmers and solve real-world development hurdles.

### Our Mission

- **Developer First:** Creating tools built by developers, for developers.
- **Open Source:** We believe in sharing knowledge and the power of the community.
- **Efficiency:** Transforming complex workflows into elegant, simple solutions.
